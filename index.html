Welcome to Horeb Restaurant

Horeb Restaurant brings the vibrant flavors of East Africa to Calgary. We believe in fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and warm hospitality.

Every dish reflects our heritage and the rich culture of East African cuisine. From the aromatic spices to the time-honored cooking methods, we invite you to experience an authentic culinary journey.

Our passion is to share the warmth and flavors of our homeland with every guest who walks through our doors. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience.
